Business Management Suite for Makeup Artists

A comprehensive full-stack solution developed for a professional makeup artist to manage her brand and clients. Customers can book services, schedule virtual consultations via Calendly, explore a portfolio of past work, and make secure payments. The admin dashboard streamlines operations with booking management, invoice generation, earnings reports, and business analytics — helping turn a creative practice into a scalable business.

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap
  • Backend: PHP, MySQL
  • Features: Service booking system, virtual consultations, portfolio gallery, payments & invoicing, earnings & reports, admin dashboard
  • My Role: Full-stack developer — responsible for frontend design, backend logic, database modeling, and integrations
